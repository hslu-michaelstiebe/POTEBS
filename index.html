About the Project

The POTEBS project investigates the potential of E-Bike Sharing Systems (EBSS) in different spatial types — urban, peri-urban, and rural — within the Basel metropolitan area. By analyzing user characteristics and behavior patterns of both free-floating and station-based e-bike systems, the project aims to provide critical insights into how these systems can be better integrated into multimodal transport networks.

Partners: University of Basel, Pick-e-Bike AG, PubliBike AG

Grant: Swiss Federal Office of Energy (SFOE), Grant No: S/502720-01

Duration: Dec 2023 to Jan 2027
