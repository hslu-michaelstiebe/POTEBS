§ 03 · FINDINGS

What the Data Says.

I.

Extreme concentration, near-50% dormancy.

A minority of registered users produces the overwhelming majority of trips, while almost half complete no trip at all. Gini = 0.77.

Concentration
II.

Green values do not predict intensive use.

Pro-environmental self-identity reduces power-user likelihood once context is controlled. Power users are utility-optimizers, not ideologues.

Against narrative
III.

Substitutes transit — not cars.

Shared e-bike trips most often replace public transport and walking. Direct car-replacement accounts for a small minority of substitutions.
